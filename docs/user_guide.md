# User Guide

## Getting Started

1. Open the app at `http://localhost:8501`
2. Click **Create Account**, enter your name, email and a password
3. You are now logged in

## Step 1 — Risk Questionnaire

Navigate to **AI Advisor** in the sidebar.
Answer 5 questions about your age, investment horizon, income stability, loss tolerance and experience.
You will receive a risk score (1–10) and a profile label: **Conservative**, **Moderate**, or **Aggressive**.

## Step 2 — Build your Portfolio

Navigate to **Portfolio**.
Enter a stock ticker (e.g. `AAPL`), the number of shares you hold, and your average buy price.
Click **Add**. Repeat for each holding.

> Tip: use at least 2–3 holdings to unlock portfolio optimization.

## Step 3 — Optimize

Still in the Portfolio page, click **Run Optimization**.
The system fetches 1 year of historical price data and computes the allocation that maximizes your risk-adjusted return.
The result shows suggested weights (%), expected annual return, volatility, and Sharpe ratio.

## Step 4 — Get AI Advice

Navigate to **AI Advisor** and click **Generate AI Advice**.
The AI analyses your current allocation against your risk profile and returns 3 paragraphs:
- Assessment of your current portfolio
- Rebalancing suggestions
- Market outlook for your profile

Previous advice sessions are saved and can be reviewed in the same page.

## Step 5 — Explore the Market

Navigate to **Market**.
Type any ticker (stocks, ETFs, crypto like `BTC-USD`) and select a time period.
The page shows key metrics (P/E, 52-week range) and an interactive price chart.
